Fashion

The world of fashion takes a lot of space in the minds of people, especially celebrities. People are interested in the clothes of celebrities, just like people are interested in their artworks and creations, where you find a lot of articles and discussions about the clothes of an important person on an occasion; fashion is a world that has evolved over the years to become one of the most important worlds of our time.

History of the world of fashion

The world of fashion and clothing refers to the vast world of apparel and accessories that Charles Frederick Worth began in the 19th century who was the first fashion designer to put his brand on the clothes he made. Who was nicknamed the Minister of Fashion, was the designer of the Queen of France Marie Antoinette, until the revolution took place in France and moved to London
